Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious lasagna. It's a classic recipe that everyone should know.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1 (26 oz) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (8 oz) box lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 (8 oz) container mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat until no longer pink. Drain and return to skillet.
  2. Season the ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika.
  3. Spread 1/2 cup spaghetti sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Top with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles. Spread with 1/3 of the remaining spaghetti sauce, 1/2 of the ricotta cheese, and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese.
  5. Repeat layers twice. Top with remaining 3 uncooked lasagna noodles and remaining spaghetti sauce.
  6. Cover with aluminum foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray, or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and bake an additional 5 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
