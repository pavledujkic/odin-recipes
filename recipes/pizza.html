Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious pizza. It's a classic recipe that everyone should know.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup pepperoni slices
  • 1/4 cup diced green bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough onto a pizza pan.
  3. Spoon the pizza sauce over the pizza dough.
  4. Top the pizza with mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, pepperoni slices, diced green bell peppers, diced red bell peppers, and sliced black olives.
  5. Bake the pizza for 12 to 15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted.
