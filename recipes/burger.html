Burger Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a juicy, tender burger. It's a classic recipe that everyone should know.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp paprika

Steps

  1. Season the ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 patties.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the patties and cook for 4 minutes on each side, or until done.
